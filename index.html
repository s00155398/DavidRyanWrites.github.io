An Introduction

David is an aspiring writer with a penchant for the fantastic, the miserable, and dinosaurs.

He’s been writing fantasy, sci-fi, and speculative fiction for six years.

David has a Masters of Art in Creative Writing, and is an English and History teacher by trade.
